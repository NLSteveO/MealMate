## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
2. Put butter in a big glass pan and place in oven to melt while preheating
3. Blend together eggs, flour, milk, sugar (optional), salt, and vanilla
4. Pour batter into the hot glass pan with the melted butter
5. Bake for 13 minutes

## Notes

- Sugar is optional
- The pancake will puff up dramatically in the oven and then deflate - this is normal
- Serve with lemon juice and powdered sugar, syrup, or fresh fruit
