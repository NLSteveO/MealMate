## Instructions

1. Brown the meat then set aside to drain
2. Dice the onion to the size you like and cook with the rum or wine in the pot you'll be using for your chili
3. Add in the garlic halfway through cooking the onion
4. Blend one can of the diced tomatoes, then add it along with the other unblended can to the onions and garlic
5. Stir in all the spices (chili powder, cayenne, basil, parsley, salt, tabasco)
6. Add the green pepper and stir
7. Add the browned meat and stir
8. Add all the beans and mushrooms, stir everything together
9. Bring to a simmer then cook on low for at least 1 hour (2 hours if you like)

## Notes

- Ground meat amount can be reduced to preference
- Mushrooms are optional
- Use dark rum (not spiced) or red wine - just enough to cook the onion until soft
- Blending one can of tomatoes gives the base a thicker consistency
