## Instructions

1. Heat waffle iron
2. Beat eggs with hand beater in medium bowl until fluffy
3. Beat in remaining ingredients just until smooth
4. Pour batter from cup or pitcher onto center of hot waffle iron
5. Bake about 5 minutes or until steaming stops
6. Remove waffle carefully

## Variations

- **Cheese and Bacon**: Omit salt. Stir 1 cup shredded sharp cheese (4 oz) and 8 slices bacon (crisply cooked and crumbled) into batter
- **Corn Waffles**: Omit salt. Substitute 1 cup cornmeal for 1 cup of the flour and 1 can (8 oz) cream-style corn for 3/4 cup of the milk. Stir in a few drops red pepper sauce
- **Nut Waffles**: Pour batter onto center of hot waffle iron. Immediately sprinkle about 2 tablespoons coarsely chopped or broken nuts (toasted if desired) over batter

## Notes

- Can substitute whole wheat flour for all-purpose
- Can use melted butter or margarine instead of vegetable oil
- If using self-rising flour, omit baking powder and salt
- Makes twelve 4-inch waffle squares or three 9-inch waffles
