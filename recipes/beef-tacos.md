## Instructions

1. Dice onion and mince garlic
2. Brown ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat
3. Add onion, cook until softened, about 3 minutes
4. Add garlic, cumin, and chili powder, cook 1 minute
5. Pour in diced tomatoes, simmer 5 minutes until thickened
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste
7. Warm tortillas in a dry pan or microwave
8. Serve beef mixture in tortillas topped with cheese, lettuce, and sour cream

## Notes

Make a double batch of the meat for easy leftovers. Great over rice too.
