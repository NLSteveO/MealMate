## Instructions

1. Dice the onion
2. Dice the roma tomatoes
3. Remove seeds from jalapenos if you want less heat, then dice
4. Roughly chop the cilantro
5. Combine everything in a bowl
6. Squeeze the lime juice over top
7. Add salt to taste and mix

## Notes

- Adjust jalapenos to your heat preference - leave seeds in for more kick
- Best served fresh, but keeps in the fridge for a day or two
- Great with chips, tacos, fajitas, or on top of grilled meat
