## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
2. Pat wings dry with paper towel
3. Season how you like
4. Arrange in a single layer on a lined baking sheet
5. Bake 20 minutes on the first side
6. Flip and bake 15 minutes on the second side
7. Toss in your sauce of choice or serve dry

## Notes

- Pat them dry for crispier skin
- Add your favorite wing sauce after baking (buffalo, BBQ, honey garlic, etc.)
- Works well with a wire rack on the baking sheet for extra crispiness
