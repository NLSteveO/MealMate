## Instructions

1. Combine sugar and cornstarch until smooth
2. Add vinegar, water, ketchup, salt, and oil
3. Cook over medium heat until mix comes to a boil and thickens
4. Stir constantly

## Notes

- Can substitute white sugar for brown sugar
- Great with chicken, pork, or as a dipping sauce
