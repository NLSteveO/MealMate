## Instructions

1. Dice onion and mince garlic
2. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat
3. Add ground beef, break apart and cook until browned
4. Add onion, cook until softened, about 5 minutes
5. Add garlic, oregano, and basil, cook 1 minute
6. Stir in diced tomatoes and tomato paste
7. Simmer on low for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally
8. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package directions
9. Season sauce with salt and pepper to taste
10. Serve sauce over spaghetti, topped with parmesan

## Notes

Sauce freezes well for up to 3 months. Add a splash of red wine for extra depth.
