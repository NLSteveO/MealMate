## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350F. Spray 13x9-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray
2. Melt butter in medium saucepan over medium heat
3. Add onion and garlic, cook and stir until onion is tender
4. Add flour, cook and stir 1 minute
5. Gradually whisk in chicken broth, cook and stir 2-3 minutes until slightly thickened
6. Add cream cheese, stir until melted
7. Stir in 1/2 cup of the shredded cheese, chicken, chiles, and pimentos
8. Spoon about 1/3 cup mixture onto each tortilla, roll up and place seam-side down in baking dish
9. Pour remaining mixture evenly on top and sprinkle with remaining 1.5 cups cheese
10. Bake 20 minutes or until bubbly and lightly browned
11. Sprinkle with cilantro and serve with salsa

## Notes

- Use rotisserie chicken for a quick shortcut
- Can prep ahead and refrigerate before baking
