## Instructions

1. Cook rice according to package directions
2. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces and season with salt
3. Slice bell peppers and cut broccoli into florets
4. Mince garlic and ginger
5. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat
6. Cook chicken until golden, about 5-6 minutes, then set aside
7. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until crisp-tender
8. Add garlic and ginger, cook 30 seconds until fragrant
9. Return chicken to wok, add soy sauce, toss to combine
10. Serve over rice

## Notes

Great for meal prep - keeps 3-4 days in the fridge. Can substitute tofu for chicken.
