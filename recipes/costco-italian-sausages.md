## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. Line a baking sheet with foil or parchment
3. Place sausages on the sheet with space between them
4. Bake for 15-18 minutes
5. Flip sausages halfway
6. Continue baking another 15-18 minutes (35 minutes total)
7. Check internal temperature reaches 165F

## Notes

- Costco Kirkland brand Italian sausages
- Sweet spot is around 35 minutes total
- Serve in buns, sliced over pasta, or alongside peppers and onions
