## Instructions

1. Lay out wraps and spread cream cheese evenly on each
2. Layer sandwich meats (turkey, ham, chicken - use whatever you like)
3. Slice cucumber and bell pepper, halve cherry tomatoes, slice avocado
4. Arrange veggies on top of the meats
5. Drizzle with olive oil
6. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes
7. Roll up tightly and slice in half

## Notes

- Costco sells a trio pack of turkey, ham, and chicken deli meats
- Use whatever sandwich meats you prefer
- Can slice into pinwheels for a party appetizer
