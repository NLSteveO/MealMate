## Instructions

1. Slice chicken into thin strips
2. Mix cumin, chili powder, salt, and juice of 1 lime for marinade
3. Toss chicken in marinade, let sit 10 minutes
4. Slice bell peppers and onion into strips
5. Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat
6. Cook chicken until browned and cooked through, 5-6 minutes
7. Remove chicken, add peppers and onions, cook until slightly charred
8. Add minced garlic, cook 30 seconds
9. Return chicken to pan, squeeze remaining lime over top
10. Serve in warm tortillas with sour cream

## Notes

Add guacamole and salsa for extra toppings. Works great with steak too.
