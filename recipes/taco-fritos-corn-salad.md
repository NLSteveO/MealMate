## Instructions

1. Drain corn and mexi corn
2. Slice cherry tomatoes in half
3. Chop red bell pepper and slice scallions
4. Combine corn, mexi corn, tomatoes, bell pepper, scallions, lettuce, and cheddar in a large bowl
5. Make the dressing: mix mayonnaise, sour cream, lime juice, salsa, and half a packet of taco seasoning
6. Add dressing to the salad and toss to combine
7. Sprinkle a little more taco seasoning on top
8. Add Fritos right before serving for maximum crunch

## Notes

- Add the Fritos at the very last minute so they stay crunchy
- Works with different chip flavors too (Doritos, etc.)
- Great for parties and Super Bowl snacking
