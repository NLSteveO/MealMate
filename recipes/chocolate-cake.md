## Instructions

### Cake

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. Grease and flour pans very well
3. Combine flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, salt, and baking powder
4. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla and mix
5. Beat for 2 minutes
6. Add 1 cup boiling water and mix
7. Pour into prepared pans
8. Bake at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes
9. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out on rack

### Chocolate Buttercream Icing

1. Beat butter until smooth
2. Gradually add icing sugar, cocoa, and vanilla
3. Slowly add milk until you reach desired consistency (may only need a few tbsp)

## Notes

- Batter will be thin after adding the boiling water - this is normal
- Can use margarine instead of butter for the icing
- Cocoa amount for icing can range from 1/4 to 1/2 cup depending on how chocolatey you want it
