## Instructions

1. Use day-old rice, or cook rice and spread on a sheet pan to cool
2. Dice carrots, mince garlic and ginger, slice green onions
3. Beat eggs in a small bowl
4. Heat vegetable oil in a wok over high heat
5. Scramble eggs quickly, break into small pieces, set aside
6. Add more oil if needed, stir-fry carrots 2 minutes
7. Add peas, garlic, and ginger, cook 1 minute
8. Add rice, stir-fry 3-4 minutes until heated through
9. Add soy sauce, toss to coat evenly
10. Return eggs to wok, add sesame oil and green onions, toss and serve

## Notes

Day-old rice works best - fresh rice can get mushy. Add chicken or shrimp for protein.
