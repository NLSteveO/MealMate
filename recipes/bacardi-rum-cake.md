## Instructions

### Cake

1. Preheat oven to 160C (325F)
2. Grease and flour a 4L bundt pan or 3L tube pan
3. Sprinkle nuts over bottom of pan
4. In a large mixing bowl combine cake mix, pudding mix, eggs, rum, oil, and water
5. Using an electric mixer, beat at low speed until moistened
6. Beat at high speed for 2 minutes
7. Pour batter over nuts
8. Bake for 1 hour or until a skewer inserted in the cake comes out clean
9. Cool 15 minutes, then turn onto a serving plate

### Glaze

1. Melt butter in saucepan
2. Carefully stir in sugar, water, and rum
3. Boil for 5 minutes, stirring constantly
4. Remove from heat
5. Prick top of cake with fork
6. Spoon and brush glaze evenly over top and sides
7. Allow cake to absorb glaze, repeat until glaze is used up

## Notes

- The glaze soaks in better when the cake is still warm
- Keeps well for several days - the flavor improves over time
- Can also keep some glaze to serve over slices
