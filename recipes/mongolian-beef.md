## Instructions

1. Toss beef strips in cornstarch until evenly coated
2. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat
3. Sear beef in batches until crispy on the outside, set aside
4. In the same pan, add minced garlic and ginger, cook 30 seconds
5. Add soy sauce, beef stock, and brown sugar, stir to combine
6. Bring sauce to a simmer until it starts to thicken
7. Return beef to the pan, toss to coat in sauce
8. Add sliced green onions, toss and serve over rice

## Notes

- Recipe from MikeFit on TikTok - may need to rewatch video for exact technique
- The cornstarch coating is key for crispy beef
- Serve over steamed rice
